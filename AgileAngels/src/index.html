THE AGILE ANGEL'S DICTIONARY

Essential terms redefined by women who actually test the code

"It works on my machine"

Translation: I didn't test it anywhere else and I'm hoping you won't either.

"Minor UI issue"

What the dev calls it when the entire page looks like a Picasso painting on mobile.

"Can't reproduce"

Developer magic spell that makes bugs invisible — but only when they're watching.

"Quick fix before release"

Famous last words. The fix that spawns 3 new bugs and a mandatory overtime pizza night.

"The requirements were clear"

Narrator: They were not. The PM wrote them at 2 AM and even they can't explain them now.

"Edge case"

What devs call the scenario 80% of real users encounter. We call it 'Tuesday'.

"Behind every successful software release, there's a QA woman who found 47 bugs the devs said didn't exist."

— Ancient QA proverb, circa last Tuesday's standup
